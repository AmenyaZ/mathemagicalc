Algorithm for converting any decimal number to a hexadecimal number

This algorithm is valid for both whole numbers and fractions

Given a decimal number(N)
Case A: number is a fraction( N < 1)
Multiply the fractional part ( i.e integer part = 0) by 16.
Divide the integer portion of the product obtained by 16 and store the remainder

Case B: number is an integer (N >= 1)
divide the number by 16 and store the remainder.

treat the divisions as integer divisions
Repeat step 1 until the number(n) becomes zero

Note: the remainders obtained in step 2 are the hexadecimal numbers so replace 10 - 15 with A - F
